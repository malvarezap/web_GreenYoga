About me

Hi, I am Triin. I love Green and I love Yoga. Green as a colour of balance and harmony, Green as leaves in nature, Green as a colour of the Sea somewhere far far away, Green as all the most healthiest vegetables, green as a colour of heart chakra, loving, joyful, magical element between the body and mind. My heart it open, my mind is free as i think of Yoga. It's in me and with me as I flow through the Life with smile.
I am qualified 200 RYT in Vinyasa Flow Yoga. My style is fluid, nourishing, playful focusing on deeper meaning of each asana, not just performing poses but feeling them with every cell of my body. I love exploring the world of pranayama, chanting, meditation and visualisation. Always feeling the rhythms in me and in nature.
